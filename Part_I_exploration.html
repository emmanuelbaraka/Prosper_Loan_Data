Conclusions¶

in conclusion, the status of the loans. that is iof one will pay and how sooon they will pay depend on several factores which affect one another. this include the individual earnings, their score and alpha ratings. people who earn more tend to have high prosper ratings and good alpha ratings of AA, A, or B. those who earn less tend to have poor alpha ratings and low prosper ratings. this variables all together tell how likely onme is to paying off their loans in time. those with high income, good alpha ratings and high prosper ratings are most likely to pay off their loans in good time while those with low earnings, poor alpha ratings and low prosper rating are more likely to go beyong the required time to pay their loans or even default.

to reach to these conclusion some steps had to be taken. this include ordering the categorical data to make the visualisations easy to read and undestand. removing all the null values in tne dataset to avoid having a kisleading visualization caused by the null values and finally some viasualizations had yo be adjusted on the scale to make them fit well in the plotting grid.
